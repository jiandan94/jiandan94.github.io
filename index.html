About me

Status: Lecture at National Center for Applied Mathematics in Chongqing
E-mail: qkai94@163.com
Address: Huixian Building, CHongqing Normal University

Previously, I received my B.S. and Ph.D. degrees in College of Mathematics and Statistics from Chongqing University (CQU) in 2017 and 2023, respectively.

Research interests

My current research focuses on the interface of statistical regularization theories and machine learning. Several interested topics include support vector machine, feature selection, dimension reduction and data mining.
